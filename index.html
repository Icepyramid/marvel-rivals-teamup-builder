How to navigate
  • Click a hero (sidebar, board, panel, or comp) to select it.
  • Drag any hero icon from anywhere and drop it exactly on the board to place it there.
  • Drag a board icon to move it; drag it onto a comp slot to add it to your team.
  • Right-click a board icon to remove it (and anything left link-less).
  • Left-click a link to recolor it (first swatch resets to green); right-click a link to hide it, right-click the same spot again to bring it back; Reset colors reverts every link to green at once.
  • The very first hero placed reveals its full web in colorless "preview" links — click an icon to confirm its link.
  • Empty comp slot: click it (arms it — next hero click fills it), or select a hero first and click the slot.
  • Filled comp slot: click to bring it onto the board; select a different hero first and click to replace it; right-click to clear it.
  • Scroll to zoom the board; drag empty board space (left-click or middle-click) to pan.
  • Middle-click and drag (the mouse's 3rd/scroll-wheel button) to scroll the hero list, the detail panel, this controls panel, and any other scrollable list — an alternative to hunting for a scrollbar.
  • The detail panel offers the 3 shortest loops back to a hero — show any of them (each in its own color, independently closable).
  • Auto arrange lays the board out with clean horizontal/vertical/diagonal lines; Back / Forward undo and redo any action.
  • Full loop theory draws a verified minimum-loop cover of every eligible hero at once, generously spaced, and opens a panel on the right explaining the graph theory behind it. Click again to return to your board.
  • Comp-only view clears the board down to just your team comp's icons and the links between them — still fully interactive (drag/remove as normal). Click again to bring your board back exactly as it was. Neither this nor showing/hiding a shortest loop ever resizes or re-fits the view.
  • Hold right-click and drag over empty board space to draw a selection box — every icon inside it gets selected (shown with a blue ring), along with the links between them. Left-click-drag any one of them to move the whole group together; right-click any one of them to remove the whole group at once. A plain right-click (no drag) on empty space clears the selection.
  • The detail panel's Show all partners / Show all enhanced buttons toggle — click again to remove exactly the heroes/links they added (anything still needed by another open loop, route, or the other bulk button stays).
  • Route finder picks a start and end hero and offers the 3 shortest Team-Up routes between them — show one to highlight it on the board in its own color.
  • Boards (top of the sidebar): keep multiple separate boards side by side, each with its own layout + team comp. + New starts a fresh empty one, the dropdown switches between them (auto-saving the one you're leaving), Save stores the current board's layout + team comp in this browser, Delete removes the current board (you always keep at least one).
  • Quick save / Quick load (toolbar): a faster checkpoint for just the board's hero layout and links — it never touches your team comp and never moves or resizes the view, unlike the sidebar's Save/board-switching.
  • 📷 Board image downloads the board diagram (with arrows showing who enhances whom) plus a simple row of your team comp's icons underneath. 📷 Comp image downloads just the team comp, arranged in a ring so the arrows between your 6 heroes are easy to read. Neither is a raw screenshot — both are cleanly redrawn for sharing.
  • Drag the small handle above Team comp up or down to resize how much of the workspace it takes.
